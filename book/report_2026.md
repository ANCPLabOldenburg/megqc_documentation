# HTML reports

Every different type of analysis generates its own HTML report. Each report is divided in several tabs covering the different metrics for different type of channels (MAG or GRAD) and even tasks. The figures in these reports are quite interactive and you can zoom in and out, revel legends on hovering, hide or show specific epochs or channels...

Covered metrics: *here i'm planning to explain the metrics as they'll appear in all reprots*
1. Standard Deviation of the Data
2. Peak-to-Peak (manual calculation)
3. ECG (Electrocardiogram) calculation
4. EOG (Electrooculography) calculation
5. Muscle Artifacts calculation
6. Power Spectral Density
